Free dictations to improve your English

This tool is here to help you throughout your journey with the English language. The goal is to provide you with the bare minimum to remain independent of AI and do your own business. No translators but access to a dictionary (wiktionary). Double-click on a word and a modal shows you the definitions. There are different types of topics to have a lot of vocabulary.

You can use offline access for your training, but if you leave the page or refresh it, you lose your progress. With connected access, you save your progress and see where you left off, what you've completed, and what you haven't touched yet.

It is a simple tool which can be complementary to your studies but it is not there to teach you anything. To make writing easier, you can avoid punctuation (which is given to you when you validate a segment). But you should capitalize words that start a sentence, even if they're in the middle. A help feature is implemented, you can click the reveal button to get the very next letter but you can only use it three times per segment.
