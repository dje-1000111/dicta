About

This app is for people who want to practice and improve their English.

So improve your understanding but also your transcription, your writing, your reading, your syntax, your grammar. It is also the possibility of learning some phrasal verbs placed in a specific context.

To have a large amount of content, there are different topics written mainly by the author of the video.
Most videos are automatically generated based on author-written subtitles and YouTube timestamps.

Others (not so many) are written by me from YouTube's automatic transcription (and therefore timestamps are also manual).

Please, feel free to contact me if you would like to make any suggestions.
